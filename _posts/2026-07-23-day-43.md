  Today I learned about green tea and its potential health benefits while also focusing most of my time on presenting my project. I learned that green tea contains natural antioxidants called catechins, which may help reduce inflammation and protect cells from damage caused by free radicals. It also contains caffeine and L-theanine, a combination that can improve alertness, concentration, and mental focus without producing the same level of jitteriness as coffee. Researchers have also studied green tea for its potential role in supporting heart health, metabolism, and overall wellness, although its benefits are most effective when combined with a healthy lifestyle. Learning about green tea gave me a better understanding of how natural compounds in foods and beverages can contribute to maintaining good health. It also reminded me of the importance of evaluating scientific evidence when learning about nutrition and health-related topics.

Overall, today helped me build both my scientific knowledge about green tea and my confidence in presenting technical information to an audience.
