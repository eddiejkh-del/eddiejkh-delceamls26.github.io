I also explored the Random Forest Regressor, an ensemble learning method that combines multiple decision trees to improve prediction accuracy and reduce overfitting. Through this research, I gained a stronger understanding of how different regression models are evaluated and compared, as well as the importance of selecting appropriate performance metrics. These concepts will be valuable as I continue developing and analyzing predictive models throughout my research project.
